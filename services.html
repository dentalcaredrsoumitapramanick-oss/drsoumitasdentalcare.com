Our Comprehensive Dental Services

From routine preventive care to specialized endodontics and cosmetic enhancements, we have your smile covered.

  • Preventive
  • Restorative
  • Cosmetic
  • Pediatric
  • Endodontic
  • Surgery & Gum Care

A. Preventive Dental Services

Protecting your smile for a lifetime of health through regular checkups and education.

  • Routine dental exams and checkups
  • Professional teeth cleaning (prophylaxis)
  • Dental X-rays (bitewing, panoramic, etc.)
  • Fluoride treatments & Dental sealants
  • Periodontal (gum) evaluation
  • Patient education & oral hygiene instruction

B. Restorative Dental Services

Restoring the strength, function, and aesthetics of damaged or missing teeth.

  • Tooth-colored fillings (composite resin) & Amalgam fillings
  • Dental crowns (porcelain, zirconia, metal, etc.) & Dental bridges
  • Inlays and onlays
  • Full and partial dentures
  • Dental implants and implant restorations

C. Cosmetic Dental Services

Enhance your confidence with a brighter, straighter, and more beautiful smile.

  • Teeth whitening / bleaching
  • Porcelain veneers & Cosmetic bonding
  • Smile makeovers
  • Gum contouring / reshaping

H. Pediatric Dentistry

Gentle and friendly care for our youngest patients to ensure a positive start to oral health.

  • Child dental exams and cleanings
  • Fluoride treatments & Dental sealants for kids
  • Pulp therapy (baby root canal)
  • Habit counseling (thumb sucking, pacifier)

I. Endodontic (Root Canal) Therapy - SPECIALIST CARE

Expert-level care for complex tooth infections, led by Endodontist Specialist Dr. Soumita Pramanick.

  • Root canal treatment
  • Retreatment of failed root canals
  • Apicoectomy (root-end surgery)
  • Prosthodontic Services (Bridges and crowns on implants)

Other Specialized Services

Periodontal (Gum) Care

  • Scaling and root planing (deep cleaning)
  • Periodontal maintenance
  • Pocket reduction surgery
  • Bone grafting and regenerative procedures

Oral Surgery & Sedation

  • Tooth extractions (simple and surgical) & Wisdom tooth removal
  • Bone grafting / sinus lifts
  • Frenectomy (tongue or lip tie release) & Biopsies and lesion removal
  • Oral sedation & IV sedation (where appropriate)
